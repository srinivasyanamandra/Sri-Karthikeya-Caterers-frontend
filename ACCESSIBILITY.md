# Accessibility Statement

## Our Commitment

Srikarthikeya Caterers is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

## Conformance Status

This website aims to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.

## Accessibility Features

### Navigation
- ✅ Keyboard navigation support
- ✅ Skip to main content link
- ✅ Consistent navigation structure
- ✅ Clear focus indicators

### Content
- ✅ Semantic HTML structure
- ✅ Proper heading hierarchy (H1-H6)
- ✅ Descriptive link text
- ✅ Alt text for all images
- ✅ Clear and simple language

### Visual Design
- ✅ Sufficient color contrast (4.5:1 minimum)
- ✅ Text resizable up to 200%
- ✅ No information conveyed by color alone
- ✅ Responsive design for all devices

### Forms
- ✅ Clear labels for all form fields
- ✅ Error messages and validation
- ✅ Keyboard accessible
- ✅ Required fields clearly marked

### Multimedia
- ✅ Text alternatives for images
- ✅ No auto-playing content
- ✅ Pause/stop controls where needed

## Known Limitations

We are aware of the following limitations:
- Some third-party content may not be fully accessible
- Older browsers may have limited support
